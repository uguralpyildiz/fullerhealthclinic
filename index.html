What We Do

Our Expertise in Advanced Hair Transplantation

At Fuller Clinic, we specialize in the most advanced and effective hair restoration methods available today. Our team applies cutting-edge techniques tailored to your unique hair structure and aesthetic goals. Learn more about our core procedures below.

Sapphire FUE

Sapphire FUE is a next-generation FUE method that uses sapphire blades for creating channels, resulting in faster healing and more accurate graft placement.

Get Detailed Information

FUE – Follicular Unit Extraction

FUE is the most common and minimally invasive method in modern hair transplantation. The donor area is shaved before the procedure, allowing for precise graft harvesting and natural-looking results.

Get Detailed Information

DHI – Direct Hair Implantation

DHI involves directly implanting the harvested follicles using a specialized implanter pen, offering more control over direction, depth, and density of each graft.
